About Me

Hello, I'm Rhea-Anne Danao, but you can call me "Eyya."

I was born and raised in the province of Isabela, where I spent most of my childhood surrounded by nature, simplicity, and strong community values.

My journey took an unexpected turn when an anonymous person gave me the opportunity to continue my studies in Makati. Moving from a small town to the bustling city was a significant challenge, but it also opened doors to new experiences, opportunities, and personal growth.

I have always been passionate about technology and problem-solving. My curiosity about computers led me to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, where I gained hands-on experience in programming, software development, and system analysis. I enjoy learning new things, whether it's coding in Java, designing user-friendly interfaces, or researching the latest tech trends.
